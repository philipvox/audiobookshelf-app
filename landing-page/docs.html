Everything you need to know to set up and use Secret Library with your Audiobookshelf server.

Getting Started

Secret Library connects to your personal Audiobookshelf server. Here's how to get up and running in minutes.

Prerequisites

  • An Audiobookshelf server (self-hosted or cloud)
  • Your server URL (e.g., https://abs.yourdomain.com)
  • Your login credentials

Setup Steps

1

Download the App

Download Secret Library from the home page. Android is available now, iOS coming soon.

2

Enter Your Server URL

On the login screen, enter your Audiobookshelf server address. Include https:// if your server uses SSL.

3

Sign In

Enter your username and password. These are the same credentials you use to log into your Audiobookshelf web interface.

4

Start Listening

Your library will sync automatically. Browse your books, download for offline listening, and enjoy.

Features

Secret Library is designed for the best possible audiobook listening experience.

Offline Mode

Download books to listen anywhere without an internet connection.

Sleep Timer

Set a timer or let it stop at the end of the current chapter.

Variable Speed

Adjust playback speed from 0.5x to 3x with fine-grained control.

Chapter Navigation

Jump between chapters easily with the chapter list view.

Bookmarks

Mark your favorite passages and return to them anytime.

Progress Sync

Your listening progress syncs automatically with your server.

Queue Management

Build a queue of books to listen to next.

Car Mode

Android Auto support for safe listening while driving.

Playback Controls

  • Skip Forward/Back: Tap to skip 30 seconds, or customize in settings
  • Scrubbing: Drag the progress bar to seek to any position
  • Per-Book Speed: Each book remembers its own playback speed
  • Smart Rewind: Automatically rewinds a few seconds when you resume after a pause

Library Organization

  • Browse by author, narrator, series, or genre
  • Filter by in-progress, finished, or not started
  • Search across your entire library
  • View detailed book information and descriptions

FAQ

What is Audiobookshelf?

Audiobookshelf is a free, open-source audiobook and podcast server. You install it on your own hardware (or a cloud server) and it organizes and streams your personal audiobook collection. Secret Library is a mobile client that connects to your Audiobookshelf server.

Is Secret Library free?

Yes, Secret Library is completely free with no ads, subscriptions, or in-app purchases.

Do you have access to my audiobooks?

No. Secret Library connects directly to your personal server. We never see your server address, your library, or any of your data. Everything stays between your device and your server.

Can I use this without my own server?

Secret Library requires an Audiobookshelf server to connect to. If you don't have one, you'll need to set one up first. Check out the Audiobookshelf documentation for setup guides.

When is iOS coming?

iOS is in development and coming soon. Follow our updates for release announcements.

How do I download books for offline listening?

Open any book and tap the download icon. The book will be saved to your device and available without an internet connection. You can manage downloads in Profile > Downloads.

Does my progress sync between devices?

Yes. Your listening progress syncs to your Audiobookshelf server, so you can switch between devices and pick up where you left off.

Troubleshooting

Can't connect to server

  • Make sure your server URL is correct and includes https:// or http://
  • Check that your server is running and accessible from your network
  • If using a local IP address, ensure you're on the same network as your server
  • Try accessing your server URL in a web browser to verify it's working

Audio not playing

  • Check your device volume and make sure it's not muted
  • Try closing and reopening the app
  • If using Bluetooth, ensure your device is properly connected
  • Try a different audiobook to see if the issue is file-specific

Progress not syncing

  • Ensure you have an internet connection
  • Check that your server is accessible
  • Progress syncs automatically in the background; give it a moment
  • Try manually pulling down to refresh on the library screen

Downloads failing

  • Check that you have enough storage space on your device
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Try downloading over WiFi instead of cellular data
  • Large audiobooks may take some time; check the Downloads screen for progress

App crashing

  • Make sure you're running the latest version of the app
  • Try clearing the app cache in your device settings
  • Restart your device
  • If problems persist, try uninstalling and reinstalling the app

Still need help?
